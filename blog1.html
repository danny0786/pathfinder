What are multiple natures

dont talk to her, her nature is not good? ?he has such a lovely nature, he always helps people? We all have heard many such situations in our lives, somewhere. But have we ever tried to find that actually this nature means? Very few of us. But those who know respect people of all kinds of nature because no matter what kind of a nature they possess, they have a quality , a special talent that defines him/her. So what actually Human Nature is? Human Nature is basically a carton full of characteristics including how a person thinks or how and what he feels or how he/she acts which humans are said to have. We all meet different people in our life in our day-to-day life, do all behave the same with us? No, because each and every person we meet have a different way of seeing the world, it can be like us and also just opposite the way we think about life or see the world. It is said one's personality is shaped by combination of nature and nurture influences. So your personality, your reaction to any situation completely depends on the nature he/she has. There are different types of nature humans have So the different types of human natures are,
1) Protective Nature: The people with protective nature like to follow rules and also make others follow the rules. These people like to identify and rectify the errors. These are the people who come to rescue when someone is in danger and stand up for the weak or abused or those who can?t protect themselves.
2) Educative Nature: These are the people who explain things very clearly and precisely and try to understand why others were not able to understand. They try to simplify the concepts by giving examples and analogies. For them it?s a pleasure when others learn.
3) Administrative Nature: People of this kind set goals and make plans to achieve them. They follow a proper schedule and regularly review their progress. They are the people who delegate responsibility to others.
4) Creative Nature: These are people with really very innovative thoughts. They usually absorb themselves in creative pursuits. They can solve typical problems easily as they look at situations or problems very differently than others.
5) Healing Nature: These are the people who help others in staying healthy and fit. They love to take care of the people when they are not well and look into the health of elderly family members.
6) Entertaining Nature: People having entertaining nature capture and hold people?s attention. They can amuse people and make them laugh and always be the centre of attention.
7) Providing Nature: These people love to serve others by doing small things like remembering their birthday or anniversary and giving gifts. They volunteer social causes like donating money and personal items to the needy.
8) Entrepreneurial Nature: These people are very careful while spending money or their budget and try to spot ideas that can make money. They can start ventures by bringing together various resources.
9) Adventurous Nature: They love playing adventure sports and taking risks whether it is socially, physically, mentally or financially.
